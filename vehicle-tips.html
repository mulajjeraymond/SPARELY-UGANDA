Why Engine Air Filters Matter

A clean air filter keeps dirt and debris out of your engine, improving fuel efficiency and performance. Experts recommend replacing the air filter every 15,000–30,000 km. Learn how to check and change it yourself in minutes.



The Importance of Engine Coolant

Coolant regulates engine temperature and prevents it from freezing or overheating. Low or dirty coolant can lead to engine failure. Discover how to check coolant levels and when to flush and refill your cooling system.

Ignoring warning lights, skipping oil changes, using the wrong fuel—these are just some mistakes that reduce your engine’s life. Learn what to avoid and how to build better habits for a reliable vehicle.

A glowing check engine light can signal anything from a loose fuel cap to a failing catalytic converter. Don’t ignore it! We explain the common causes and what steps to take when this light appears on your dashboard.
